Phase 1 - Customer Complaint & Resolution Tracking System 

1. Introduction 

1.1 Project Title 

Customer Complaint & Resolution Tracking System 

1.2 Project Description 

The Customer Complaint & Resolution Tracking System is a centralized web-based application designed to help organizations efficiently manage, monitor, and resolve customer complaints and service issues. 

The platform enables customers, support teams, service agents, supervisors, and administrators to log complaints, assign issues, track progress, communicate updates, and ensure timely resolution of customer concerns. 

The system improves customer satisfaction, operational efficiency, accountability, and service quality by maintaining a structured workflow for handling complaints from initiation to closure. 

The application can be used in industries such as: 

Telecom  

Banking  

Retail  

E-Commerce  

Healthcare  

Logistics  

Education  

Utility Services  

IT Support Services  

2. Business Need 

Organizations often struggle with customer complaint management because complaints are tracked manually using emails, spreadsheets, or disconnected systems. This creates several operational challenges: 

Delayed issue resolution  

Poor complaint visibility  

Missed SLA commitments  

Lack of accountability  

Duplicate complaint handling  

Inefficient communication with customers  

Difficulty tracking complaint history  

Low customer satisfaction  

Without a centralized complaint tracking system, organizations cannot effectively monitor complaint trends, agent performance, or service quality. 

The Customer Complaint & Resolution Tracking System addresses these challenges by providing a unified platform for complaint registration, workflow management, escalation handling, resolution tracking, and reporting.
